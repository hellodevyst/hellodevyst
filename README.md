## Hey, I'm Faizan

I'm a full-stack engineer who started building products for other people and eventually started building a company around it.

I founded **[Devyst](https://devyst.com)** because I kept seeing the same pattern: businesses getting sold AI and software that looked good in demos but fell apart in production. I knew how to build things that don't, so I did.

Being a full-stack engineer shapes how I run things. I care about architecture before features. I care about what ships over what sounds impressive. And I believe the best product decisions come from people who actually understand the code underneath.

<br />

## What I believe

- Software should solve a real problem or it shouldn't exist
- AI is a tool, not a strategy
- The best teams ship working software early and improve it honestly
- Simplicity is harder than complexity and worth the effort
- A good system runs without you — that's the goal

<br />

## Devyst

A technology studio building AI products, custom software, and automation for businesses that want to grow without adding unnecessary complexity.

We design from scratch. We ship in weeks. We don't disappear after launch.
